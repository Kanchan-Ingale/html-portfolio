My Top 3 Movies of all the time

Uri : The Surgical Strike

It ia a military action film based on true events. It depicts the retaliatory strikes carried out by the Indian Army on terrorist launch pads in POK after a deadly terrorist attack on an Indian army base. The film follows the planning, execution, and aftermath od the surgical strikes showcasing the courage and determination of soldiers involved.

The Ghazi Attak

It is an Indian war film based on true events of the mysterious sinking of the Pakistani submarine PNS Ghazi during the 1971 Indo-Pak war. The movie portrays the underwater battle between the Indian Submarine INS Karanj And the Pakistani submarine PNS Ghazi. The film captures the tension, strategy and heroism displayed by the naval forces during the conflict. It is a gripping tale of courage and determination set against the backdrop of historical events.
